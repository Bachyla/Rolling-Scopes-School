History

It's just a story about a little boy who was sent to the village, as it usually happens, to collect potatoes. Unfortunately, the village is not far from Chernobyl, and this year there were many Caloradic beetles on the field. Will our hero be able to collect all the potatoes and not be bitten by big bugs?

How to Play

Press the up, down, left and right arrow keys on your keyboard, or W.A.S.D. keys to move the player. Do your best to harvest all potato bushes without being taken down by a bug! Get to the other side of the road to level up!

You have 3 lives. Clearing a level is worth 600 points, and a potato bushes of any height is worth 300.

Use the skills to kill beetles. But do not forget about the cost of spells. If you go into a minus, you will get 0 points for a potatoes score.
